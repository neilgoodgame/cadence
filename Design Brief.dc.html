FITNESS DATA VIEWER & ANALYSIS

A pro-grade home for every FIT file — analysed, browsed, and built.

A multi-sport analytics workspace for triathletes and coaches. Ingest activities from Garmin Connect and Zwift, dissect them with power, heart-rate and heat-strain analytics, and build structured bike & run workouts — all on top of your normalized FIT data model.

Clean · data-dense Light + dark Desktop-wide Multi-sport
01
Context &
assumptions
Who it's for
Multi-sport athletes & coaches who already live in TrainingPeaks / Garmin / Strava and want deeper, faster analysis without the noise.
Sources
Garmin Connect FIT exports (garmin-sync) and Zwift virtual rides. Drag-drop or auto-pull the latest activity.
Data spine
Built on the FIT message schema — Activity → Session → Lap → Record — persisted into a fully normalized relational database.
What's distinctive
Power & HR duration curves, L/R balance, and heat-strain zones — analytics your fit-analyser already computes, surfaced visually.
Data visualization
Charts built with D3 — the PMC (fitness/fatigue/form), log-scale power & HR duration curves, time-in-zone, and hydration trends. In React, use D3 for scales & shape math (d3-scale, d3-shape) and let React render the SVG.
